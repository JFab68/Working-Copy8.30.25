Healthcare Failures

Critical

Medical neglect and inadequate healthcare delivery leading to preventable deaths and constitutional violations across Arizona's prison system.

34,500+ People Affected
Multiple Preventable Deaths

Specific Healthcare Issues:

  • Delayed Medical Care: Critical delays in emergency medical response and routine healthcare delivery
  • Inadequate Staffing: Chronic shortage of qualified medical professionals in correctional facilities
  • Mental Health Crisis: Limited access to mental health services and crisis intervention
  • Medication Management: Inconsistent medication distribution and monitoring
  • Preventable Deaths: Multiple deaths that could have been prevented with proper medical oversight

Impact: The Jensen v. Shinn lawsuit specifically cites healthcare failures as constitutional violations affecting thousands of incarcerated individuals.
