Medical Neglect Crisis

Arizona prisons have the highest death rate of any state prison system. Inmates die from treatable conditions like diabetes, heart disease, and infections at rates 3x higher than the general population.

Source: Arizona Department of Corrections mortality data, 2023

Constitutional Violations

Federal courts have found Arizona's prison system violates the 8th Amendment prohibition against cruel and unusual punishment through inadequate medical care, excessive heat, and inhumane conditions.

Source: Parsons v. Ryan federal lawsuit findings

Taxpayer Waste

Arizona spends $1.2 billion annually on corrections, yet recidivism rates remain among the highest in the nation. The system fails both public safety and fiscal responsibility.

Source: Arizona Joint Legislative Budget Committee, 2024
